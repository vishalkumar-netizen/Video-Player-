🎬 Netflix Style Local Video Player

Select Your Local Video


📖 How to Use

1. Click on "Select Your Local Video" and choose any video from your computer.

2. (Optional) Upload subtitle file (.vtt or .srt).

3. Use playback speed buttons (1x, 1.5x, 2x).

4. Adjust volume from dropdown.

5. Enjoy watching without installing any software.

Note: Works directly in browser. No data is uploaded to server.
